## Summary — Update 1 North Star

**Headline:** A large "make it prettier + cozier" update. No new locations or new mechanics — take the existing six-location Chapter 1 and make it look, sound, and feel much nicer.

**What we're doing**
- **Visuals (top priority):** Big polish on the canvas art across all six locations. Keep the hand-drawn canvas technique, make it much richer. Mix cool rainy tones outside with warm glow from lights/windows. Add a title/start screen.
- **Weather:** Richer rain, puddles, lightning, mood — varied feel is welcome.
- **Audio:** Add full sound this update — background music plus sound effects and rain.
- **Pacing:** Slow things down a bit; current pace feels too fast for a cozy game.
- **Puzzles:** Keep rotate-path repairs, but tune a difficulty ramp — easy in scene 1, getting harder each scene, hardest in the last (Rainbarrel Row). Overall vibe stays relaxing.
- **Story/dialogue:** Light polish of existing lines, no big expansion.
- **Ending:** Make the Chapter 1 "complete" summary screen feel rewarding.
- **Robot companion:** Keep simple.

**Constraints / scope guards**
- Stick with the current 6 locations and route.
- Keep browser saving as-is and working.
- Keyboard only (no touch this update).
- Hints/tutorial stay minimal.
- No accessibility work this round.
- Cozy & calm is the target feeling throughout.

**Timeline:** No rush — quality over speed.

**Workflow:** Claude batches related work by area (e.g. art, then weather, then audio), verifies each works, then Chris playtests.
